Improving workplace efficiency

At work, new tech is a game-changer. It makes tasks faster, teams more connected, and projects super efficient.

  • Automation: Machines handle boring tasks.
  • Better communication: Tools like video calls keep teams connected.

Risks in the Workplace

But it's not perfect. Relying too much on tech can cause issues too.

  • Job loss: Automation might replace workers.
  • Data security: Hackers could steal important info.

Impact on personal relationships
